About This Innovation

AI-Powered Plant Disease Detection System

This invention leverages advanced Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to automatically identify plant diseases from leaf images. Developed by researchers at Lovely Professional University, our system provides farmers with fast, accurate, and low-cost disease diagnosis.

Key Features:

  • Real-time disease identification
  • Severity estimation and progression analysis
  • Treatment recommendations and preventive measures
  • Multi-crop and multi-disease support
  • Offline functionality for remote areas
  • Adaptive learning mechanism
